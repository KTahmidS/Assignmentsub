About Our Platform

Our website is a streamlined platform for university students to submit lab reports and assignments online. Students can register, upload their files, and submit them before the deadline, ensuring timely submissions. Organized by department, the system makes it easy for instructors to manage and grade assignments. Teachers can review submissions, provide feedback, and assign grades efficiently. The platform supports multiple file formats and ensures data security. With a user-friendly interface and seamless access, it simplifies academic submissions for both students and faculty.
